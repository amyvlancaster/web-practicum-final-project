Your First Pet!

Congratulations! We are so happy that you have decided to embark on your journey to owning your very first pet, and we are so excited to be a part of it. Here are some general guidelines to keep in mind while you are preparing for your very first furever friend.

  • Be sure to invest in pet-proofing your home before your new pet arrives. This means making sure there aren't any poisonous plants or things that can be chewed on and destroyed. Check for small areas where animals may get stuck, and seal them accordingly.
  • Create areas in your home that are clearly meant to be "their space". This means setting up cozy beds in every room, litter boxes that are easily accessible but still private, and toys or scratching posts in places that encourage pet use.
  • If you live with someone else, make sure they approve of you bringing home a new pet. The last thing you want is to find out they are allergic, or that they can't afford the potential pet fee renting may incur. Also be sure that everyone in the house is willing to take some responsibility for the animal, and talk through scheduling walks, feeding time, cleaning, etc.
  • Be stocked up on the essentials! Wet food, dry food, litter, collars, leashes, toys, treats, cleaning supplies, etc. You want your pet to come home all prepared and ready to go!
