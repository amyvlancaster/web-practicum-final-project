How to Adopt

Step One

Meet the Critters

Browse online or come into our shelter and get a chance to meet all the wonderful pets we have available! You can ask for one-on-one time, take dogs for a walk, or cuddle with our cats in their cat sanctuary. We have smaller pets available as well!

Step Two

Application and Approval

Once you've found the one, fill out the appropriate application in-person or online. We will help you every step of the way should you have any questions.

Step Three

Take Them Home!

Congratulations! You've officially received your new friend! Ask us for help on what supplies you need, and get your car and home prepared to bring home your new friend. It's that simple!
