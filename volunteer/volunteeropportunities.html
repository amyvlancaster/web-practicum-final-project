Help at the Shelter

Dog Walking

Want to help out your local shelter and get in a good workout? You can volunteer to walk our dogs! From letting them out in our caged-in backyard, to taking them on a hike in the trails near our shelter, you have many options on how you can spend quality time with our pups!

Feeding and Cleaning

As much as we love our critters, they can sometimes be quite messy. Volunteer to help us feed and clean our cats, dogs, and other small animals. We always need help with cleaning litter boxes, getting everyone fed at meal time, and changing out cages and kennels.

Transport Volunteering

Sometimes we will get animals from out-of-state. Volunteer to help us transport pets across the town, county, city, state, or even country! You would also be helping us pick up and transport animals from the airport.
